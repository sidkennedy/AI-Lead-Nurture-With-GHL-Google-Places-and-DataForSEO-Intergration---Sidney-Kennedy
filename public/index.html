First time? Two minutes to go live.

Just imported this from GitHub into Replit? You need to set a few secrets before the engine works. Open Tools → Secrets in your Replit workspace and add:

  1. ADMIN_KEY — the password that protects your dashboard. Pick any long random string. Required.
  2. ANTHROPIC_API_KEY — get one at console.anthropic.com. Powers all AI replies. Required.
  3. DATABASE_URL — Replit gives you a free Postgres under Tools → Database; copy its connection string here. Required.
  4. GHL_API_KEY + GHL_LOCATION_ID — your GoHighLevel sub-account credentials. Required for live SMS.
  5. DATAFORSEO_LOGIN + DATAFORSEO_PASSWORD — powers the visibility scanner (primary path). The engine sweeps the local Google Maps grid around the prospect's business and feeds the AI live competitive ranking data. Sign up at dataforseo.com. Recommended.
  6. GOOGLE_PLACES_KEY — used for prospect business lookup (name, address, reviews). Also the fallback scanner if DataForSEO is unavailable. Recommended.
  7. DEV_MODE set to 1 — turn this on while you build & test, so nothing accidentally sends real texts. Optional.
What "ADMIN_KEY" actually does: the admin URLs need ?key=YOUR_ADMIN_KEY appended (e.g. /admin?key=k9_x4vQ-8sB2). It's a single shared password — invent it, save it as a Replit Secret, then use it in every admin link. If you click "Open the Admin" without setting it first, you'll land on a guided setup page that walks you through it.

What you need to do

This codebase ships empty. You bring two things — your industry context and your conversation script — and the rest of the engine (Google Maps lookups, visibility scans, follow-up scheduler, learning brain) runs the same for everyone.

1

Set your industry

Open Setup. Tell the AI who it's texting, what your product does, and the pain points it should reference.

2

Build your variants

Open Variants. Drag together the steps of your conversation — text steps, business detection, and the final video send.

3

Wire your CRM

Point your GoHighLevel webhooks at this server. New contacts get assigned a variant and the AI takes over.
